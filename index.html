ABOUT PAL-SWISS TECH HUBS

In todayâ€™s rapidly evolving digital landscape, Switzerland faces a pressing challenge: a growing talent gap in technology-based skills that threatens to stifle innovation and economic growth. The Pal-Swiss Tech Hubs initiative offers a groundbreaking solution by connecting Swiss enterprises with Palestinian[JTM1] ICT talent, equipping businesses to thrive in the global tech industry. Importantly, this initiative is not about outsourcing; it enhances teams with skilled professionals without the typical challenges of relocation or remote hiring.

Founded by AlNayzak, an esteemed non-profit organization established in Jerusalem in 2003, which has consistently propelled educational and scientific excellence. This initiative is supported by the Repre- sentative Office of Switzerland in the oPt and the Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation (SDC). It is implemented in cooperation with other Swiss partners, like Gibellina-Arts AG, which enriches our initiative with extensive Swiss-Palestinian economic connections and Dynaskills GmbH, that enhances our efforts by matching Swiss job market demands with Palestinian ICT expertise. We are committed to advancing our shared goals of technological empowerment and sustainable growth.
